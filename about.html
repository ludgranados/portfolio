About


Hi, my name is Ludwin but friends and family know me by my middle name Abraham. I am a first generation grad student with a bachelor's in psychology, with current ABA instructor experience and previous military experience. I am fluent in both English, and Spanish. I love technology, and like helping others when I am able to. As a goal in life, I would like to help people who are in mental health distress. I also aspire to learn enough to be able to teach others ways of being able to get to where they want to be in life. I want to use my love for technology and goal of making a difference in people's lives to help to make a difference in our community.
